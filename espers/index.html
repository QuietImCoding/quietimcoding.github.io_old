Which class are you?

Class 3 Espers

  • Can detect only concious thoughts
  • Jobs available as secretaries or helpers

Class 2 Espers

  • Can detect thoughts down to the pre-concious level
  • Can notice subliminal paterns and associations
  • Jobs available as lawyers, managers, and psychologists

Class 1 Espers

  • Can detect primitive low-level urges
  • Jobs available in very high positions such as the police, government, and psychiatry

Esper Rules

Espers need to follow specific rules to stay in the Guild

  • You must marry an Esper
  • You must not talk to Espers banished by the Guild
  • You must follow all of the ethical rules outlined in the handbook
  • Swear to make all decisions for the good of the universe, including non-espers
